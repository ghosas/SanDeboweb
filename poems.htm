The Time

                                                                         24/9/2002

 

I am a warrior ,Mongol is my kind

            I fought , slayed numerous mortals

But could not win the Imortal time .

It slowed me down , cooled my veins

And wekened my muscles ,I lost my sight

 

My weak Muscles could not face the guilt

            The murders ,indiscriminate death caused

by me now haunt my soul to infinity

            My beloved one s seem to desert me

I am now a lonely  warrior thirsty for love

I murdered my dreams ,Plundered my hopes

 

            They only fear me for my vegance

And respect my anger but alas they donot see

            My mind ,the finite mortal system

I am now dying and there is no one left to cry

31/10/1998

The love which sparks in the eye and make the heart 

                            beat fast

 

The love which gives a feeling of being  in heaven with her

She is a beauty in her self a beauty which blinds

                                     a person

 

 with her   eyes which seems to reflect the sky

                                                                                            Her Black hair shows as if the glittering of gold

Her mind is pure as pure as it is to a mortal is water

                                                      but poisons the mind

My First poem

10/11/1998

On this beautiful evening in the hills

I remember of my first poem

written in an evening like this

My first living dream was composed.

It was given words to be described

 

I neither thought to be a poet

nor to be a soldier 

the only thing I thought of is my love

with a shadow of her

engraved on paper by me

 

Now I am old and aged

with wrinkles in my face and cracks in my mind

I cannot see the nature 

but I can sense it with my soul

which reminds me of her

my long forgotten love

Poems

09/10/2003

Once I was god and created 

the poem for humanity

Sun used to set and moon used to rise

but my poems used to be new

A new poem on every full moon

 

Days used to pass ,nights used to flow

my poems become more more and more

many autumns passed away 

many summer scorched away 

and with that my youth dried away

 

Then one day I questioned ,"What am I doing?

Who will read these when I pass away 

then I searched the eternity 

and found whom I want

She was there  statue in time

 

She stands in time or time stands in her

a mysterious fog conceals my mind 

I see the future but it is dark

and in the morning as the sun rises 

my life dries up

 

I see dark in my eyes and the future is near

I draw my sword and plunge it

Deep into her heart

As the blood gushes out 

I wake up from my dreams only to feel alone

You

13/10/2003

 

I saw the moon I saw the sun

                                                I saw everything a man can see

Yet I could not see you

                          You were not there in my among the Mortals

 

I searched the hill I searched the sea

                               I searched everything a man can be

Yet I could not feel you 

                                 You were not in my dreams

 

I felt the air ,I felt the the fire 

                                      I felt everything a man can desire

Yet I could not feel you 

                                        You were not there in my sense

 

I loved the birds I loved the tree

                            I loved everything there can be

Yet my love I could not love you

                                You were not there in my love
